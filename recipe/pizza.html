Pizza

Description

This pizza is Mara's favorite as it is is rich with colorful ingrediants. The flavors create a bomb in the palate.

Ingredients

  • 1 ready made pizza crust
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup fresh sliced mushrooms
  • 4 cups of jalapeno
  • 2 cups of fried bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate
  2. Roll out prepared pizza dough to a size that will fit the grill
  3. Place on grill for 5 mintues and flip over
  4. Add the sauce, cheese, green bell pepper, mushrooms, bacon, and jalapenos
  5. Cover the grill and llow to cook over high heat for 5 to 10 minutes
