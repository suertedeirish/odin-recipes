Ranch Pork Chops

Description

Pork Chops with an an ease of the Air Fryer and a Ranch twist. The combination of ranch and air fryer gives these pork chops tenderness and juiciness.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, center-cut pork chops, 1-inch thick
  • Cooking Spray
  • 2 teaspoons dry ranch salad dressiong mix
  • Aluminum Foil
