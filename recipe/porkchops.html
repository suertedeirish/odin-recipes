Ranch Pork Chops

Description

Pork Chops with an an ease of the Air Fryer and a Ranch twist. The combination of ranch and air fryer gives these pork chops tenderness and juiciness

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, center-cut pork chops, 1-inch thick
  • Cooking Spray
  • 2 teaspoons dry ranch salad dressiong mix
  • Aluminum Foil

Steps

  1. Place pork chops on a plate and lightly spray both sides with cooking spray
  2. Sprinkle both sides with ranch seasoning mix and let sit at room temperature for 10 minutes
  3. Spary the basket of the air fryer with cooking spary and preheat the air fryer to 390 degrees F
  4. Place chops in the preheated air fryer
  5. Cook for 5 minutes. Flips chops and cook for 5 minutes more
